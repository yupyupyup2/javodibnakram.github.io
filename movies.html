My Favourite Movies

The First of Them:

Movie's Title: the Dark Night

Movie's Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Movie's Release Date: 18 July 2008 (USA)

Short Description: When the menace known as the Joker emerges from his mysterious past, he wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham. The Dark Knight must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.




The Second of Them:

Movie's Title: Team Khan

Movie's Genre: Documentary, Sport

Movie's Release Date: 16 November 2018 (USA)

Short Description: Fly-on-the-wall documentary about professional boxer Amir Khan. Filmed over two years, it follows Amir and his team in their quest to fight the best boxer on the planet, the unbeaten Floyd Mayweather Jr.




The Third One:

Movie's Title: The Last Race

Movie's Genre: Documentary, Sport

Movie's Release Date: 16 November 2018 (USA)

Short Description: THE LAST RACE is the portrait of a small-town stock car racetrack and the tribe of blue-collar drivers that call it home, struggling to hold onto an American racing tradition as the world around them is transformed by globalization and commercialization.




A Final One:

Movie's Title: Green Book

Movie's Genre: Biography, Comedy, Drama

Movie's Release Date: 21 November 2018 (USA)

Short Description: A working-class Italian-American bouncer becomes the driver of an African-American classical pianist on a tour of venues through the 1960s American South.
